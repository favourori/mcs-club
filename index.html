ABOUT US
Mathematics and Computer Science Club (MCSC) at the campus of Southern Arkansas University is a student organization with a membership open to anyone, but thatâ€™s specifically intended for students in the math and computer science programs. The objective of this club is to promote interest and education in math and computer science by providing a comfortable environment for students to pursue and promote their passions. Club members have opportunities to build their resumes for graduate school and the work force. Additionally, the club provides a place for those interested in these fields to come together and form networking relationships. The club meets regularly during the school year. Club services are initiated by the student members and are available weekly at no cost. We hope to play a larger role in the community through bringing the love of math and computer science to children and adults everywhere. We hope to produce many communal service projects to better our community and ourselves with the youth of today.
